Easy Lasagna

Description

Making the perfect homemade lasagna should not have to be a hard task. That's why we have a top-rated easy lasagna recipe that comes together quickly and has has a short budget friendly ingredient list.

Ingredients

  • Beef
  • Spaghetti sauce
  • Cheeses: You'll need cottage cheese, mozarella, and Parmesean
  • Eggs
  • Seasonings
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Water

Steps

  1. Cook and drain the ground beef, then stir in the spaghetti sauce and simmer.
  2. Combine the cottage cheese, 2 cups of mozarella, eggs, half of the Parmesean, and seasonings.
  3. Assemble the lasagna according to the detailed recipe.
  4. Bake, covered, for 45 minutes.
  5. Uncover and continue baking for 10 minutes.
